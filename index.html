PENS

In a world increasingly dominated by touchscreens and keyboards, pens remain timeless. They've been around for centuries, and they’re not going anywhere anytime soon. Whether you’re writing on paper, crafting handwritten letters, or making a quick note in your planner, a pen is often the tool of choice. Pens also hold sentimental value, whether it's a gift from a loved one, a special pen used for signing a contract, or the pen you used to write your first novel.)

Pens may not be as flashy as the latest smartphone or as quick as a digital tablet, but they’re undeniably cool because they never lose their charm. Whether you’re in a meeting, at school, or out in a café, a good pen is always in style. So next time you pick up a pen, remember: it's not just a writing tool, it’s a way to make your mark on the world.)
